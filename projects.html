Buster's Pizza Sales Report System

Recent (2025)

This project was developed at my university as part of a collaborative effort with two team members. The objective was to create a digital system for Buster's Pizza to log their sales reports, addressing the inefficiencies of their outdated method of recording data using pen and paper. By implementing this system, we aim to streamline the centralization of their data while reducing human errors in input and calculations. As part of this group project, my key contribution was designing the user interface (UI) for the Login Page and Admin Dashboard.
